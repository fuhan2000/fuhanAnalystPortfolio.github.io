Filtering of data by year

import pandas as pd
df.Start_Time = pd.to_datetime(df.Start_Time)
df_2016 = df[df.Start_Time.dt.year == 2016]
df_2017 = df[df.Start_Time.dt.year == 2017]
df_2018 = df[df.Start_Time.dt.year == 2018]
df_2019 = df[df.Start_Time.dt.year == 2019]
df_2020 = df[df.Start_Time.dt.year == 2020]

Accident in Cities

Los Angeles, Miami increase rapidly from Yr19 to Yr20. Miami has the most accidents in Yr20 (29576), followed by Los Angeles(24183) and Charlotte(15454).

Seattle manage to keep the accident counts low over a sustained period of more than two years. There is a slight drop from Yr17 to Yr20. Seattle has the lowest accident of 3081 in Yr20 out of this list of sixteen cities. Oklahoma City has the second lowest at 5525.

There are cities that show a gradual increase in accidents like Atlanta and Austin. This could be because of increase in number of vehicles

The graph do not include data in Jan16. So this may affect cities with low 2016 accidents like Baton Rouge, Nashville, Oklahoma City, Portland and Raleigh.
